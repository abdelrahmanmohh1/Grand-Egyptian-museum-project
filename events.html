Grand Hall

The Grand Hall is an awe-inspiring space that showcases the grandeur of the GEM's architecture. With its towering ceilings, imposing designs, and beautiful natural light, it is the perfect venue for hosting events that require a touch of sophistication and elegance. This space can accommodate up to 2,000 people, making it an ideal location for large events such as concerts, exhibitions, and gala dinners. The versatility of the Grand Hall means that it can be easily transformed to suit the needs of any event. From exclusive events to high-endgatherings, the Grand Hall at the Grand Egyptian Museum offers a one-of-a-kind setting that will leave a lasting impression on all those who attend.
For event inquiries or bookings, please contact events@gem.eg
